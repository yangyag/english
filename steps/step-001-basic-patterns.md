# Step 001 - 기본 문장 엔진: want to / need to / can

Status: Completed
Created: 2026-05-15
Completed: 2026-05-15

## 목표

가장 기본적인 문장 엔진 3개를 익힌다.

- I want to + 동사원형
- I need to + 동사원형
- I can + 동사원형

## 핵심 패턴

### 1. I want to + 동사원형

뜻: 나는 ~하고 싶다

예문:
- I want to study English.
- I want to read English documents.
- I want to speak English naturally.
- I want to improve my vocabulary.
- I want to understand long sentences.

### 2. I need to + 동사원형

뜻: 나는 ~해야 한다 / ~할 필요가 있다

예문:
- I need to study English every day.
- I need to learn more words.
- I need to understand English documents.
- I need to practice speaking.
- I need to read error messages.

### 3. I can + 동사원형

뜻: 나는 ~할 수 있다

예문:
- I can study for 30 minutes every day.
- I can read simple English.
- I can understand some words.
- I can write short sentences.
- I can practice after work.

## 주의

- want study X → want to study O
- need study X → need to study O
- can to study X → can study O

## 숙제 첨삭 요약

1. i want to read english documents.
   - Correct: I want to read English documents.

2. i have to learn words more.
   - Better: I need to learn more words.
   - 또는: I have to learn more words.

3. i can study english 30 minutes every day.
   - Correct: I can study English for 30 minutes every day.

4. i want to speak english by foreigners naturally.
   - Correct: I want to speak English with foreigners naturally.
   - Better: I want to speak naturally with foreigners.

5. i have to understand long sentence.
   - Correct: I need to understand long sentences.

6. i can study english by work out.
   - Correct: I can study English after work.
   - Note: 퇴근 후 = after work

7. i have to practice grammar.
   - Correct: I need to practice grammar.

8. i want to understand english news.
   - Correct: I want to understand English news.

9. i can short sentence that english.
   - Correct: I can write short sentences in English.

10. i want to my idea in english.
    - Correct: I want to express my ideas in English.
    - 쉬운 버전: I want to say my ideas in English.

추가 문장:
- 제출: i often read english documents because i am a programmer.
- Correct: I often need to read English documents because I am a programmer.
